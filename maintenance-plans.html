Maintenance

Maintain a Cleaning Schedule

From Driveways to Parking lots 2Kings Asphalt has a plan for you!   
  1. Maintaining asphalt is crucial for extending its lifespan and ensuring it remains in good condition. Here’s a comprehensive checklist for asphalt maintenance:

  4. Sweeping: Regularly sweep debris off the pavement to prevent dirt from causing cracks.
  5. Crack Repairs: Fill in cracks as soon as they appear using an asphalt repair patch.
  6. Pothole Patching: Repair potholes promptly with a pothole repair kit to prevent further damage.
  7. Power Washing: Pressure wash the asphalt to remove dirt and oil stains.
  8. Sealing: Reseal the asphalt every two to five years to protect it from UV damage and water penetration.
  9. Weed Removal: Remove weeds by hand-pulling or using vinegar before repairing any cracks.
  10. Winterizing: Ensure the asphalt is sealed and in good condition before winter to prevent damage from freezing and thawing cycles.
  11. Raveling: Check for disintegration of the asphalt surface and schedule repairs if needed.
  12. Rutting: Avoid parking in the same spots repeatedly to prevent wheel indentations.
  13. Discoloration: Replace old or damaged asphalt that appears dull or whiteish.
  14. Grade Depressions: Check for areas that are not level and address any depressions.
  15. By following these steps, you can keep your asphalt in top shape and avoid costly repairs12.
